Top That!

One Deck. One Winner. No Mercy.

Rules:
  • Play higher than the last card on the pile
  • 2: Reset – can be played on anything
  • 5: Copy – acts like the last played card
  • 10: Burn – clears the play pile
  • 4 of a kind – clears the play pile
  • Draw to 3 cards in hand after playing
  • Move to up cards, then down cards when hand is empty
