Top That!

One Pile. One Winner. No Mercy.

How to Play ▾
  • Game Setup
    • - Deal 3 hand cards, 3 face-up cards, and 3 face-down cards per player
    • - Remaining cards form the draw pile
  • Turn Rules
    • - A card is drawn from the pile to start the game
    • - The first player plays 1 or more cards of a higher value
    • - If the deck remains, you must draw to keep 3 cards in your hand at all times
    • - Can’t play? Take the pile and flip a new card to restart
  • Special Cards
    • - 2 resets the pile
    • - 5 copies the previous card’s value (acts as a regular 5 if it’s the first card played)
    • - 10 clears the pile
    • - Four of a kind clears the pile and does not have to be higher than the last card
  • Card Phases
    • - Play all hand cards before using face-up cards
    • - Play all face-up cards before using face-down cards
    • - If you can’t play a face-up or face-down card, take the pile and must empty your hand before resuming
  • Winning
    • - First player to empty hand, face-ups, and face-downs wins
