Three’s

Rules ▾
  1. Play higher than the top card (2, 5, 10, or four‑of‑a‑kind exempt).
  2. 2 resets – next player may play any card.
  3. 5 copies previous rank for next turn.
  4. 10 burns the pile (clears, restarts play).
  5. Four of a kind also burns.
  6. Draw to 3 cards while deck remains.
  7. When hand & deck empty, play face‑up row, then blind down row.
  8. Invalid blind flip → pick up pile. First to empty all wins.
